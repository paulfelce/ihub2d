Instructions
Click anywhere to start

Click again to draw a wall. (Note, I've tested by starting in top left and first wall is left to right.)

Right click to enter an exact (mm) measurement before starting another wall

Click below (or above) a horizontal wall for a corner and perpendicular wall.

Click in line with a wall to creating an opening and in line again to continue the wall after the opening

Note, you can't create a corner from an opening

Press delete to delete a wall(can't delete the first wall)

When you get back to the start point , click the green square to complete the diagram. (Once clicked, you can no longer edit)

Click Save to "Save the current diagram"

Click "choose file" to select a file to load. Then Load . This needs a bit of work but should be sorted very soon.
